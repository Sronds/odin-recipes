Moroccan Lentil Recipe

Home Page Youssef's Special Dish

Description

This delicious meal is one that I grew up eating on a weekly basis. It's packed with protein, spices and everything nice ;). It's quick and easy to make and is great choice if you're on a budget but still want to have a tasty meal that's healthy and nutritious.

Ingredients (2 Servings)

  • Carrots (100g)
  • Onions (100g)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Tomato purée (100g)
  • Lentils (250g)
  • Herb Mix (50g)
  • Spice Mix (2 spoonfuls per spice)

Step-by-Step

  1. Chop up all vegetables into finely diced pieces.
  2. Put all chopped vegetables in a pot and add 1l water.
  3. Add tomato purée, herbs and spices to the pot.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.
  5. Stir the contents on the pot every few minutes.
  6. When the lentils look ready, add salt and pepper and serve immediately.

You're all set! Enjoy the meal.
